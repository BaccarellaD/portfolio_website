About Daniel

Daniel Baccarella is a Computer Scientist that works with a team of Game Development professionals to design and code commercial Virtual and Augmented Reality games. He is excited to help make the future of entertainment and computer interaction a reality.

Daniel has worked at Cemtrex as a game programming intern, where he gained experience in VR and mobile game development, and human computer interaction via development of Quazar. A cross platform game for the Cemtrex Smart Desk and Oculus Go mobile VR headset. He is currently working with the Cemtrex team to develop their first desktop VR game based on the Starforce book series.

Daniel has experience working with multiple programming languages, including Java, C and C#. He is an active participant in many hackathons where he works cooperatively in small teams under very tight deadlines to create interesting and creative projects.

He holds an Associates Degree in computer science from Suffolk Community College and is actively working on a Bachelors in Computer Science from Stony Brook University, with an expected graduation date in winter semester 2019.
