What It Is

Quazar is a game developed for the Oculus Go and Cemtrex Smart Desk as part of my internship.

It is currently out on the Oculus Go store and is being shipped on every Smart Desk as part of the included software suite.

What I Learned

Quazar was the first commercial game I made in Unity, and took 6 months to develop.

During that time I worked cooperatively with several programmers and artists to make the game into a reality.

Working with the team was a great way to practice a professional workflow. Everyday in the mornings we would hold meetings and discuss the game and it's features. Working cooperatively led to some really cool features, like tempest style geometric maps and brick-breaker power-ups that can be used by both the player and AI.
